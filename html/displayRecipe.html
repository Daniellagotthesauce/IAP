Creator: John Doe

Duration: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

★★★★☆

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Gradually add milk and mix until smooth.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes until golden brown.

Serving Tips

Serve warm with a drizzle of honey or a side of butter.
